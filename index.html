History of Cestas

The district is located along the road to Arcachon between Croix d'Hins and Toctoucau.

A monument near the Pierroton crossroads celebrates the work of Jules Chambrelent, an engineer from the Ponts et Chaussées, who experimented on his 500-hectare property in Saint-Alban, located a few hundred meters from there, with techniques for cleaning up and developing the wet moorland, notably by sowing pine trees.

In Pierroton, you will find the "forest-wood campus" of the INRA, the "advanced forestry biotechnology pole" of the FCBA, the headquarters of the forestry cooperative Alliance Forêts Bois and a soccer club.
